# Find XOR of numbers from the range [L, R] where L <= R    

Given two integers L and R, the task is to find the XOR of elements of the range [L, R].  
E.g.  
```
L = 5,  
R = 9,  
Expected output = 5 ^ 6 ^ 7 ^ 8 ^ 9
```
